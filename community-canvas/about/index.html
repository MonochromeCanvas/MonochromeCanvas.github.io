About The Project

A public archive for artwork made on saved paper from the Monochrome Canvas studio.

A public archive made from saved studio scraps

The Recycled Studio Paper Archive turns paper offcuts, test sheets, and saved scraps into a shared record of artists, clients, families, and collaborators connected to the studio.

Studio Practice

Fine art paper, given another life.

Monochrome Canvas works with artists, editions, portfolios, and meaningful printed objects. The saved studio paper carries that same attention: material that might have been discarded becomes a small invitation to make something new.

Artist Community

A gallery for the people connected to the studio.

This archive is meant to feel generous and public without becoming anonymous on the back end. Artists may share a name, handle, website text, location, note, or endorsement, and they may also choose to publish anonymously.

How It Works

Submit one image, then the studio reviews it.

Submissions do not appear automatically. Monochrome Canvas reviews each image before publication so the archive stays appropriate, focused, and worthy of the artists represented here.
